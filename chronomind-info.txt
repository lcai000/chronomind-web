Chronomind is a mobile dementia care simulation app that trains family caregivers and students through authentic, interactive scenarios. 

By practicing decisions in a safe environment, users learn crucial skills rather than real-life caregiving scenarios


CURRENT ISSUES:
Lack of training for current dementia caregivers
This also leads to caregiver stress and burnout due to underpreparedness
Overreliance on trial and error to solve issues
Underfunding in dementia/aging research
Society lack awareness on aging diseases( people think its natural and not an actual disease and therefore dont take it that seriously)
Right now there are a lot of differences between the medical standard and what to actually do
No easy way to practice dementia care skills in a low risk environment
Many People lack access to proper aging and dementia care
Current practice or simulations are too expensive

FUTURE GROWTH:

WHO IS OUR AUDIENCE:
Informal caregivers, family caregivers, and students

Chronomind isn't just an app, it's a training tool, empathy builder, and a support system. It prepares caregivers for reality, reduces burnout, and ultimately improves quality of life for people not just people with dementia but also the people around them and society as a whole.

By turning education into experience, we’re not just teaching caregiving, we’re changing how caregivers learn, cope, and connect.

Chronomind seeks to end the deficit in dementia and aging education.
